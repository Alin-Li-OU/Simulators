Open Source at Alin Li

Alin Li OÜ publishes its educational simulators under the Apache License 2.0. Every simulator is a self-contained single-file HTML document with no third-party runtime dependencies. This page is our public open-source landing point and supports our self-certification to ISO/IEC 5230:2020.

Compliance posture

First-party only. All four simulators are licensed under Apache License 2.0. They bundle no third-party open-source libraries, fonts, or assets at runtime. System fonts (Segoe UI, SF system fonts, Tahoma, Consolas) are supplied by the user’s operating system and are not redistributed by us.
